5 Ways to prevent fire


Unplug Appliances		Always unplug appliances before leaving the house. It saves energy and lessens the chance of having an electrical fire.

Blow Candles		Stop the fire on the candle before you go out of the house, or go to sleep. If you left it unattended it might burn something close to it and spread quickly.

Fire Extinguishers		Adding fire extinguishers in your house is also one of the safety precautions. In case a fire breaks out, you can put it out quickly and easy.

Learn how to use Fire Extinguishers		Before using a fire extinguisher, you have to know how to use it first and its purpose.

Add Fire Alarms		Adding fire alarms can be an expensive upgrade but it is a health measure for everyone living in the house. It alerts you whenever it detects smoke.
